The Vitamin Framework
Three Pillars of Development

The PDP is built around three vitamin categories — Team, Skill, and IQ. Each one holds up to five focused vitamins, keeping plans specific, actionable, and easy for athletes to absorb.

Team Team Vitamins
Focus areas related to how the player contributes within the team system — spacing, communication, rebounding, and effort plays that lift the whole group.
Driving Spacing 50/50 Balls Rebounding
Skill Skill Vitamins
Individual technical skills the athlete is developing — shooting mechanics, ball-handling, footwork, and finishing at the rim. Tied directly to drill work.
Shooting Form Off-Dribble Finishing Footwork
IQ IQ Vitamins
Basketball intelligence areas — reading defenses, understanding schemes, making the right play at the right time, and executing within the team's system.
Horns Set PNR Reads Press Break Off-Ball
